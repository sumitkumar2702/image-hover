IIT Mandi

Indian Institute of Technology Mandi (IIT Mandi) is an autonomous engineering and technology university located in Mandi, Himachal Pradesh. Since the first batch of 97 students joined in July 2009, IIT Mandi has grown to currently host 125 faculty and 1,630 students who are enrolled in various programs of studies in undergraduate, postgraduate and research programmes, and 1,135 alumni.
